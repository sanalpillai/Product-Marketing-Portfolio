Impact Guru Sales Battle Deck
For hospital administrators, NGO operators, and healthcare finance decision-makers

Positioning Statement

Impact Guru is a full-stack fundraising and distribution platform purpose-built for healthcare, combining technology, traffic acquisition, and operational support to maximize net funds raised.

Key Differentiators

  • Built-in traffic and distribution engine
  • Proven revenue outcomes across thousands of campaigns
  • Healthcare-first workflows and compliance
  • End-to-end support from setup to conversion

Market Alternatives

  • DIY donation tools with no built-in distribution
  • Generic crowdfunding optimized for virality, not healthcare
  • In-house teams with long setup times

Objection Handling

  • "We can build this ourselves" → Emphasize speed to impact and existing traffic scale
  • "Fees are too high" → Reframe around net funds raised and time-to-donation
